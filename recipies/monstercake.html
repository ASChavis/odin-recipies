Monster Cake

"It is said that once one has a taste, they will never forget the sweetness of Monster Cake."

Ingredients

  • Tabantha Wheat
  • Cane Sugar
  • Goat Butter
  • Monster Extract

Steps

  1. Purchase ingredients from various vendors
  2. Find a cooking pot
  3. Place ingredients listed in cooking pot
  4. Cook
  5. Consume Monster Cake
