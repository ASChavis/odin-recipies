Wildberry Crepes

"The Wildberries in this meal are noted to be sweet and tart, while the dough is thin and springy."

Ingredients

  • Wildberry
  • Fresh Milk
  • Cane Sugar
  • Tabantha Wheat
  • Bird Egg

Steps

  1. Locate ingredients from various vendors or in the wild
  2. Find a cooking pot
  3. Place ingredients listed in cooking pot
  4. Cook
  5. Consume Wildberry Crepes
